About ATHARV :

           ATHARV is a popular tuition centre in Thillai Nagar, Trichy since 2008. Its instructor Mrs. Kumuda Rengarajan is a passionate teacher and learner. She believes that the money earned through running a business is exhaustible but the satisfaction derived out of creating many successful students remains a pleasure forever.

Why ATHARV?

• If the question is well explained, the answer is half understood.
• Importance to both objective and subjective style of questions.
• Questions and Answers are explained in a way that students have few or no doubts. Doubts can be immediately clarified during the online sessions.
• Syllabus covered in great detail.
• Regular Assessments to ensure that practising maths becomes a habit.
• Guaranteed best results for responsible students.
