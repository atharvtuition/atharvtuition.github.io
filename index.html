About Atharv :

           ATHARV means knowledgeable and meaningful and here at ATHARV tuition centre we inculcate this into our students and strive to make it a reality. An institution’s success rely upon the leader’s shoulder who moulds and carves the best out of every student. Our founder and teacher Mrs.Kumuda Rengarajan has been doing the same since 2008. We undertake expertise Mathematics classes for students of class 7 to 10. At a student-friendly environment, we provide utmost quality of education while developing the urge to learn among students. As an example, to the concept ‘learn and grow’, we have adopted Online teaching method due to the pandemic.
